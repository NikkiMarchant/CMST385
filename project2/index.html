Concept:

The type of website I will design is a portfolio for all the projects I have created.
The goal for this website is to organize my projects and create a resume for entering my field.
The intended audience is future employeers. Contact Information along with work history
and education will be provided. All projects will be listed and grouped by language in the
other pages.I will also link my Linkedin and Github above contact information.

Site Map:

The first page will have contact info and the things listed above in concpet. The following three will list all my porjects
in order from oldest to newest to show my progression. The last page will be a gallery showing screenshots of my work.
The subtopics will be my other four pages. As shown in the wireframe the navigatin bar will be: Home, Java, Python, HTML, Gallery.
The home page will start with the header and then the nav bar. From there my contact information along with a picture will be on
the left hand side. Next to that in the center of the page will be an about me section. Below this will be two different sections
one for schooling and another for work history.
